Frequently Asked Questions

Can I contribute notes or PYQs?

Yes. You can send notes, PYQs, lab files, or useful resources through email or Instagram.

Are these official university notes?

No. These are student-contributed resources meant to help with studying and exam preparation.

Is GCU Notes Portal free to use?

Yes. The platform is completely free for students.

I found a broken link or wrong file. What should I do?

Please contact us and mention the issue. We’ll fix it as soon as possible.

Can I request notes for a subject?

Yes. Reach out and we’ll try to add them if available.

Who manages this portal?

GCU Notes Portal is maintained by students to make academic resources easier to access.
